Biography

Dennis Ceylan, the writer of this 'blog', studies since 2017 to the education sculpture at Sint-Lucas Ghent. On the basis of this portfolio I give an overall picture what has already been done and what will come.

The technique used by the artist in the making now gets its shape within the oeuvre. Within the context of contemporary art I edit some clay models with my fists, as by punching it, or by flinging the clay, so as to create a nearly arbitrary shape. By applying this creates a texture where the contours of my handpalm. I need a number of people outside this technique in order to complete the picture, which as of late 2018, 2019 Iwould start with video.
These forms/images are interpretations on the passion for the man who survives in society.

Sculptures

These sculptures appear in a classical way the human body. Although the word classic is stated, I started in a classical way but the end result has a certain dualism between classical and abstract.
As we evolve within this abstract context, we alienate the human body to look for any limits.

Drawings

The drawings below show the interpretation of a model during a random pose. These views vary in terms of technique, very experimental in order to find the right medium. This technique determines the result of the drawing itself. With a graphite rod I slide so to get a volume of the contours of the skin, with panda chalk I instinctively go out on color, and so on.
